Downloading The BusKill App
===========================

The BusKill projects uses GitHub as our forge.

To download the latest version of the BusKill app, click the ``Latest`` button under ``Releases`` on our repository's home page:

 * `https://github.com/BusKill/buskill-app <buskill-app_repo_>`_

.. figure:: /images/buskill_download1.jpg
	:alt: screenshot of the buskill-app repo home page
	:align: center

	Click "latest" on our `buskill-app repo <buskill-app_repo_>`_ page

Then you'll want to click to download three files:

#. The ``SHA256SUM`` checksum file,
#. The ``SHA256SUM.asc`` detached signature file, and
#. The archive for your OS and architecture (eg ``buskill-linux-x86_64.v0.1.0.tar.bz2``)

.. figure:: /images/buskill_download2.jpg
	:alt: screenshot of the buskill-app repo release page
	:align: center

	Download ``SHA256SUM``, ``SHA256SUM.asc``, and your OS/arch-specific release files

.. note::

	If you'd like to download our source code and build our app yourself, see :ref:`build_app`

.. _buskill-app_repo: https://github.com/BusKill/buskill-app
